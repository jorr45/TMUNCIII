Welcome to TMUNC III

It is our pleasure to host the annual Trinity School Model United Nations Conference. Founded as a conference designed to help delegates within Trinity School develop their skill in public speaking and unmoderated caucus, we are expanding the conference to include other local schools.

TMUNC III will be held on February 28th, 2015, at the Trinity School.

Registration is now open.
